Community Guidelines

Welcome to the "Streets Award" community! To ensure a positive and enjoyable experience for all users, we have established these Community Guidelines. By using the "Streets Award" app ("App"), you agree to adhere to these guidelines.

  1. Respectful Behavior

    2. Be Respectful: Treat others with kindness, courtesy, and respect. Avoid any form of harassment, discrimination, or offensive language.

    Diversity and Inclusion: Embrace diversity and inclusion. Avoid making comments or engaging in behavior that may be offensive or harmful based on factors such as race, gender, religion, or background.

  2. Appropriate Content

    3. No Offensive Content: Do not post or share content that is offensive, vulgar, obscene, or harmful.

    Intellectual Property: Respect intellectual property rights. Do not post content that infringes on copyrights, trademarks, or other intellectual property.

  3. User Interactions

    4. Positive Engagement: Engage in positive and constructive interactions with other users. Avoid trolling, spamming, or engaging in disruptive behavior.

    Dispute Resolution: If conflicts arise, try to resolve them respectfully and privately. Avoid public disputes that may negatively impact the community.

  4. Reporting Violations

    5. Report Inappropriate Content: If you come across content that violates these guidelines, please report it to us through the designated reporting mechanisms within the App.

    False Reporting: Do not make false reports or engage in malicious reporting of other users.

  5. Compliance with Laws

    6. Legal Compliance: Abide by all applicable laws and regulations when using the App. Do not engage in illegal activities or encourage others to do so.

  6. Consequences of Violations

    7. Violations of these Community Guidelines may result in consequences, including but not limited to:

    • Warning messages or notifications.
    • Temporary or permanent suspension of account access.
    • Reporting to law enforcement authorities for serious offenses.
  7. Changes to Guidelines

    8. We may update these Community Guidelines from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised guidelines. Your continued use of the App following the posting of changes constitutes your acceptance of those changes.

  8. Contact Us

    9. If you have any questions or concerns about these Community Guidelines, please contact us at enquiries@streetsaward.com.
