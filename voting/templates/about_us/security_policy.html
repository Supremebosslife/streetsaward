Security Policy

  1. Introduction

    2. We have put several features in place as our commitment to maintaining the security and confidentiality of user data on the Streets Award app. This Security Policy outlines the measures we have in place to protect the information entrusted to us.

  2. Data Security Measures

    • Secured Database: User emails are stored in a secured database with access restricted to authorized personnel only.
    • Encryption: We use encryption protocols to secure the transmission of data between the app and our servers, ensuring the confidentiality of information in transit.
    • Access Controls: Access to user data is restricted based on roles and responsibilities. Only authorized personnel with a legitimate need have access to the data.

  3. User Authentication

    • Password Security: We enforce strong password policies to ensure that user accounts are protected. Passwords are stored securely using industry-standard hashing algorithms.
    • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA): Where applicable, we encourage and support the use of multi-factor authentication to add an additional layer of security to user accounts.

  4. Monitoring and Logging

    • Audit Trails: We maintain audit trails of activities related to user data access and modifications, enabling us to trace any unauthorized actions.
    • Logging: Server logs are regularly monitored for suspicious activities, and we have mechanisms in place to promptly respond to security incidents.

  5. Regular Security Audits

    • External Audits: We conduct regular external security audits to identify vulnerabilities and ensure that our systems are in compliance with industry best practices.
    • Internal Audits: Our internal security team performs regular audits of our infrastructure, applications, and processes to identify and address potential security risks.

  6. Incident Response

    • Response Plan: We have an incident response plan in place to address security incidents promptly. This includes communication protocols, investigation procedures, and mitigation strategies.
    • User Notification: In the event of a security incident that may impact user data, affected users will be notified in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

  7. Employee Training

    • Security Awareness: All employees undergo security awareness training to ensure they are aware of the importance of security and their role in maintaining a secure environment.
    • Continuous Education: We provide ongoing training to employees on emerging security threats and best practices.

  8. Compliance with Laws and Regulations

    • Legal Compliance: We adhere to relevant data protection laws and regulations to ensure the legal and ethical handling of user data.

  9. Contact Us

    10. If you have any questions or concerns about our Security Policy, please contact us at enquiries@streetsaward.com.

  10. Changes to Policy

    11. We may update this Security Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised policy. Your continued use of the app following the posting of changes constitutes your acceptance of those changes.
